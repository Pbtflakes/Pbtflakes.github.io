Shay locomotives

Some of our favorite locomotives are those produced by Lima Iron Works, based on the patents of Ephraim Shay (1839–1916). These Shay locomotives were the most successful of the various geared locomotive designs.

The drivetrain was modified from the original patent designs by Lima Iron Works, mainly to feature shorter gearing. This increased torque at the wheels, making Shays especially useful for logging and other fields which required steep ascents. This tied back to Ephraim Shay's impetus for developing a novel drive system for steam locomotives, his dealings in the logging industry.
